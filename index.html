The Franchise

About RCB

Royal Challengers Bengaluru has been one of the IPL's most-watched and most-discussed franchises since the league's first season in 2008. Built around explosive batting and a stadium that turns into a sea of red, RCB plays cricket the way Bengaluru drinks its filter coffee — strong, loud, and without apology.

Home games are played at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, a ground famous for short boundaries, flat pitches, and a crowd that never sits down. The franchise has reached multiple finals across formats, anchored for over a decade by Virat Kohli's relentless consistency at the top of the order.

What RCB has built isn't a trophy room — it's a fandom that's outlasted heartbreak after heartbreak and only gotten louder. That's the whole pitch.
